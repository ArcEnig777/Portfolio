Wars of Arcadia

A short 3-stage Tactical RPG made in Unity over the course of 6 months, inspired by classics such as Fire Emblem and Final Fantasy Tactics. It features multiple difficulty levels , a wide variety of playable units and interactive terrain. Functionality wise, the game uses a combination of the A* algorithm and a grid system to make the functionality smooth and simple. Download the source code with the final submission to try it out!

The Game Shop

Made in collaboration with Hezron Perez, Marco Maldonado, and Derek Gaura, The Game Shop is an E-commerce website dedicated to selling videogames rapidly and effectively to it's customers. With a retro aesthetic and innovative styling, our website is appealing to multiple audiences from casual to dedicated. created with Javascript, React, HTML and CSS fro its front-end, with Javascript, KnexJS and Firebase for back-end/databases. For testing/development we used ExpressJS and NodeJS to test the website locally before deploying it to Netlify. It contains admin functionalities as well. Check it out below!

Shooting Range

In this short game you will try your marksmanship against multiple targets spread across the arena to become the champion of the Shooting Range! A small proof of concept of a shooting range made from scratch with openGL Graphics using C++. Featues collsions, ray casting, textures and lightning and much more! Made together with Erasmo Sanchez and Darren Pharakone, the project is made to demonstrate our ability with OpenGL and our capability to accurately communicate and collaborate with one another.
